## Purpose

Google Flow Director OS is a local-first PWA for planning, prompting, and managing Google Flow videos. It does not generate video and does not connect to external generation services. Its job is to prepare high-quality Google Flow inputs: Flow Scenes, Hero Images, Ending Frames, Flow Prompts, QA checklists, subtitles, and Flow Export Packages.

## Positioning

Google Flow Director OS is a production operating system for Google Flow. It helps creators move from PPT or idea to a structured Google Flow production package.
